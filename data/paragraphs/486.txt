After just a couple of hundred steps, the passage meets a massive iron gate. Anticipating such surprises, you meticulously inspect the walls surrounding the gate in search of a mechanism that would open the way. However, finding it proves elusive. Could this first obstacle truly be insurmountable? Or is the gate enchanted — but then, you could attempt the only magic available to you — the magic of the Six Words. Will you turn back to explore where the passage leads in the opposite direction (210), or will you turn to magic (114)?
