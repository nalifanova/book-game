When you take the ornament, you are surprised to find that the stones feel slippery to the touch. And for good reason. Slipping out of your hands, the diadem itself fits onto your head. It feels like dozens of searing needles piercing into it. You lose consciousness. If someone had looked into the crypt five minutes after that, they would have seen the diadem back in the hands of the goddess. And at her feet lies a corpse with swollen drops of blood where the stones touched the skull...
